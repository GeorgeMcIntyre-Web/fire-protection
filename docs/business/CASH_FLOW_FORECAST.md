## KEY CASH FLOW RULES

### âœ… DO'S

1. **Always take 50% deposit**
   - Protects cash flow
   - Client commitment
   - Covers engineer payment

2. **Invoice immediately**
   - Same day as delivery
   - Don't wait
   - Follow up at 7 days

3. **Pay yourself last**
   - Business first
   - Salary after costs
   - Build buffer

4. **Keep 3-month buffer**
   - R30,000 minimum
   - Emergency fund
   - Peace of mind

5. **Track weekly**
   - Know your numbers
   - Update this forecast
   - Adjust as needed

---

### âŒ DON'TS

1. **Don't do work without deposit**
   - Too risky
   - Sets bad precedent
   - Cash flow killer

2. **Don't overspend on marketing**
   - Stick to R2k/month
   - Track ROI
   - Organic growth first

3. **Don't hire too soon**
   - Month 6 earliest
   - Part-time first
   - Prove demand first

4. **Don't pay cash for big purchases**
   - Lease if possible
   - Preserve cash
   - Flexibility

5. **Don't forget taxes**
   - Set aside 28%
   - Quarterly payments
   - Avoid penalties
